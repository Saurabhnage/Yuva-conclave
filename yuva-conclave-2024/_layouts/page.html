About Young Indians Vadodara

Young Indians (Yi) Vadodara is a part of the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) and is a movement for Indian youth to converge, lead, co-create, and influence India's future. Yi Vadodara engages young Indians in various initiatives that promote leadership, entrepreneurship, and social responsibility.

About Yuva Conclave

The Yuva Conclave is an annual event organized by Young Indians Vadodara. It brings together young leaders, entrepreneurs, and change-makers from various fields to share their experiences, insights, and ideas. The conclave features keynote speeches, panel discussions, workshops, and networking opportunities, aiming to inspire and empower the youth to take on leadership roles and contribute to the nation's development.
